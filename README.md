## About

**Creator's World** is a collaborative community built for creators who love to build, experiment, and ship. From web development and coding to games and open-source projects, this is a space where creativity meets real-world execution.

Beyond being a community, Creator's World also delivers **professional services**, including web development and technical solutions for startups and enterprises. We believe in learning by building, growing through collaboration, and contributing back to the open-source ecosystem.

> [!IMPORTANT]  
> This is the official open-source codebase of Creator's World. You are free to fork, customize, and adapt it to create your own community platform or product.

## Why Creator's World?

- **Creator-First Community**: A welcoming and inclusive environment where developers, designers, and creators collaborate and grow together.
- **Open-Source by Design**: A clean, modern, and developer-friendly codebase built to be extended, customized, and improved by the community.
